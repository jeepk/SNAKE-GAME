SNAKE GAME

Game Rules

The game will start automatically one you start the game. Your goal is to eat as many food(green) without touching the walls. You can control the snake(purple) using the arrows in your keyboard. Your score will appear at the top as the snake eats the food. One food is calculated as 10 points. Good Luck :)
